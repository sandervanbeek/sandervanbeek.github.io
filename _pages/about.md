Welcome! I am a third-year PhD candidate in Econometrics at the University of Groningen, supervised by prof. dr. Gerard van den Berg and dr. Tom Boot. I obtained a BSc in Econometrics and a Research Master in Economics from the University of Groningen.

I am interested in duration analysis and nonparametric estimation. My PhD research focuses on modeling and identifying unobserved heterogeneity in duration models.
